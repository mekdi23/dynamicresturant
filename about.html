Our Story

We started Mekdi Restaurant with a simple idea: to bring authentic Ethiopian cuisine to our community. What began as a small family-owned restaurant has now grown into a beloved establishment known for its delicious food and warm hospitality.

Our journey has been filled with passion for Ethiopian flavors and dedication to providing an unforgettable dining experience. From our traditional recipes passed down through generations to our commitment to sourcing the freshest ingredients, every aspect of Mekdi Restaurant reflects our love for Ethiopian culture and cuisine.

As we continue to grow and evolve, our mission remains the same: to share the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia with our guests and create memories that last a lifetime.
