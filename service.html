Kitfo

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish that consists of minced raw beef, seasoned with spices and served with injera, a type of Ethiopian flatbread. It is a popular dish in Ethiopian cuisine, particularly in the Gurage and Harari regions.

Shiro Wet

Shiro wet is a traditional Ethiopian stew made from ground chickpeas or lentils, simmered with onions, garlic, and spices, offering a flavorful and hearty vegan dish in Ethiopian cuisine.

Injera

Injera is a sourdough flatbread originating from Ethiopia and Eritrea, characterized by its spongy texture and tangy flavor, commonly used as a staple accompaniment to various Ethiopian dishes, serving as both a utensil and a food item.
